Welcome to the official website of Hamere Nohe Kidanemihret (St. Mary) Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church.

We call upon everyone to join us as we worship and glorify God according to the faith, order, and tradition of the Ethiopian Orthodox Church. This website provides information about the essential services the church provides to the community. It also contains great resources about our faith and Ethiopian tradition.

Story of My Life

I was born and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. At age eleven, I moved to Kenya with my family and lived there for five years. I relocated to Canada in 2011, and have been residing here ever since. I finished my three years of high school in 2014, and am currently pursuing a degree in CS at UWaterloo.
